Monthly Customer Enrollment Forecasting Using Time-Series Analysis

  • The organization had a different program for low-income customers which provided them with discounts and relief funds. To budget for these discounts and relief funds, the organization wanted a model which could forecast the monthly customer enrolled in the program.
  • Previous enrollment trends showed seasonality in total monthly customers for this program, which had to be taken into account by the model.
  • Used time-series model Prophet to account for seasonality in the data achieving a Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) of less than 10%.
  • Incorporated various factors, including potential policy changes, to ensure accurate future trend predictions.
  • Final results were published in an Excel dashboard which contained interactive elements to view how the forecast changed based on policy changes.
  • These projections were reviewed during monthly meetings to evaluate and optimize budget allocation for discount programs.
